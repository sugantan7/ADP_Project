Cristiano Ronaldo, born on February 5, 1985, in Funchal, Madeira, Portugal, is a legendary professional footballer
widely regarded as one of the greatest players of all time.

He began his career at Sporting CP before gaining fame at Manchester United, where he won multiple titles, including the Premier League and UEFA Champions League.
He then achieved global stardom at Real Madrid, becoming the club's all-time top scorer and winning numerous awards, including four Ballon d'Or titles.
After a successful stint at Juventus, he currently plays for Al-Nassr in Saudi Arabia and continues to represent the Portugal national team.
